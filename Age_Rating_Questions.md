## Submission Process

### Step 1: Create Archive
1. Open Xcode
2. Select "Any iOS Device" as target
3. Product → Archive
4. Wait for archive to complete
5. Organizer window opens automatically

### Step 2: Validate Archive
1. In Organizer, select your archive
2. Click "Validate App"
3. Select your team/account
4. Choose automatic or manual signing
5. Wait for validation
6. Fix any errors or warnings
7. Re-archive if needed

### Step 3: Upload to App Store Connect
1. In Organizer, click "Distribute App"
2. Select "App Store Connect"
3. Click "Upload"
4. Select your team
5. Configure distribution options:
   - [ ] Upload symbols: YES (for crash reports)
   - [ ] Manage version/build number: YES
   - [ ] Strip Swift symbols: YES (for smaller download)
6. Review summary
7. Click "Upload"
8. Wait for upload to complete (5-30 minutes)

### Step 4: Wait for Processing
1. Log into App Store Connect
2. Go to your app → Activity tab
3. Wait for build to process (can take 1-2 hours)
4. You'll receive email when processing is complete
5. Build appears in "Build" section when ready

### Step 5: Complete App Store Listing
1. Go to your app version page
2. Fill in all metadata
3. Upload screenshots
4. Upload app icon (1024x1024)
5. Select build from dropdown
6. Complete privacy questions
7. Complete content rights
8. Set pricing (Free)
9. Set availability (All countries or select specific)

### Step 6: Submit for Review
1. Review all information one last time
2. Click "Submit for Review"
3. Confirm submission
4. Wait for confirmation email

### Step 7: Wait for Review
- **Expected wait time:** 1-3 days (can be faster)
- **Status updates:** Check App Store Connect regularly
- **Email notifications:** You'll receive status change emails

**Possible Statuses:**
- Waiting for Review
- In Review
- Pending Developer Release
- Ready for Sale
- Rejected (if issues found)

## If Rejected

### Common Rejection Reasons
1. **Crash on launch** - Test on physical device
2. **Incomplete information** - Double-check all metadata
3. **Privacy policy issues** - Ensure accuracy
4. **Misleading content** - Ensure screenshots match gameplay
5. **Guideline violations** - Review App Store Review Guidelines
6. **Objectionable content** - Shadow budget may be questioned
